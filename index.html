Our Story

Founded in Baltimore with a passion for exceptional tea, we've grown into the city's premier destination for artisanal teas and innovative tea experiences.

It all started during COVID when a few friends gathered at my home. I served my homemade teas, and when one friend eagerly ran to get more from the counter, everyone was amazed by the flavor. That spontaneous moment of joy during a difficult time sparked something special.
Word spread quickly, and what began as sharing tea with friends soon blossomed into a passion-driven business. So many people fell in love with our unique blends that it was clear this was meant to be shared with all of Baltimore. Every cup we serve today carries that same magic from that first gathering – bringing people together through exceptional tea experiences.
